Your questions, answered.

General Questions

What is The Hub by Salatiso?

The Hub is your secure, central dashboard for managing all the important aspects of your life, family, and business. It's a "digital homestead" that connects you to a powerful ecosystem of tools for documents (DocuHelp), finance (FinHelp), HR (HRHelp), and much more.

Who is The Hub for?

It's for everyone. Whether you're an individual planning your future, a family building a legacy, an entrepreneur launching a business, or an established company seeking efficiency, The Hub has tools to help you.

Account & Security

Is my data safe and private?

Yes. Security and privacy are fundamental to our design. Your data is stored securely, and we are committed to protecting your privacy. We will never sell your personal data. Please see our full Privacy Policy for details.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time. When you do, all of your personal data and created content will be permanently removed from our systems.

Using the Tools

Do I need to be an expert to use these tools?

Not at all. Our tools are designed to be powerful yet user-friendly. We provide templates, guides, and assessments to help you get started, no matter your level of experience.

Can I share documents or collaborate with others?

Yes. Many of our tools, especially within DocuHelp and Family Hub, are built for collaboration. You can securely share documents and control who has permission to view or edit them.

Future Plans & Pricing

Is The Hub free to use?

Currently, the entire ecosystem is in a free beta testing period. We want your feedback to make it as good as possible. In the future, we will introduce subscription and payment models, but we are committed to having powerful free tiers available forever.

How can I give feedback?

We would love to hear from you! Please use the "Feedback" or "Support" link within The Hub to share your thoughts, ideas, or report any issues.
